Magic

There are three types of magic in this world: Sun magic, Moon magic, and Star magic.

Sun mages have the ability to create fire and heat. However, this energy must come from somewhere. Sun magic slowly take energy from the environment and store it within themselves to release it in the future.

Moon mages are the opposite. While these mages have the ability to draw in large amounts of energy at once, they are unable to store it. It must be redirected. Moon mages have the power of necromancy and healing by redirecting their energy into a living or dead creature, and the power to cool the temperature or freeze by virtue of their method of gaining energy.

Star mages do not get their energy from the environment. Instead, they use their own energy to manipulate the environment around them. These mages have the power of geomancy, and even alchemy through manipulation precise enough to move individual molecules. However, since these mages do not get their energy from the environment, moving large objects is still a heavy burden.

All mages can learn any of the magic types, or any combination of the three, but it is incredibly taxing to become proficient at more than one.
