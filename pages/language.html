Drevenmüir Rvun

Drevenmüir Rvun is the language spoken by the people living on the Drevenmüir Penninsula. It is split into 3 dialects: Standard Drevenmüir, Southern Drevenmüir, and Low Drevenmüir. In addition, many important texts are still written in the literary dialect of Classical Drevenmüir.

Phrases to know for travellers!

  1. Gnacht elen - Hello
  2. De zai ölen - Goodbye
  3. Ze älojje elen - I'm sorry
  4. Schige due - Thank you
  5. Zer rvojjej - You're welcome
  6. Rvöuv pföu zacge due? - How much does that cost?
  7. Ze rvöuv zalscheñ due? - May I have that?
  8. LOCATION pföus ere? - Where is LOCATION?
